# Development roadmap

## Phase 1 — Foundation
- Product framing and architecture.
- Prisma schema and app shell.
- Dashboard experience and mock insights.

## Phase 2 — Core productivity
- Full task management flow.
- Habit logging and streaks.
- Calendar event creation and recurring patterns.

## Phase 3 — Financial intelligence
- Budgeting and transaction tracking.
- Weekly and monthly reporting.
- Savings insights and trend charts.

## Phase 4 — Engagement
- Goals, milestones, achievements, and gamification.
- Social accountability and sharing.
- AI coaching and planning recommendations.
